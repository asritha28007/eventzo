Common questions
How do I list my event on Eventzo?

Sign up as an organiser, complete your profile, and use our event creation tool. Your event will be live within a few hours after a quick review.

Is Eventzo free for participants?

Yes — discovery, saving events, and RSVP are always free. Some ticketed events may have a booking fee set by the organiser.

What cities do you currently support?

We're live in 120+ cities across India, with international expansion planned for Q3 2025. If your city isn't listed, let us know.

How does personalised discovery work?

We combine your stated interests, location, attendance history, and browsing patterns to surface events most likely to resonate with you — privately and without selling your data.
